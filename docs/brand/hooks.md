# Hooks

Travel-lines lifted from the Anti-Engineer brand brief (§7). Strategy Claude pulls from this list when writing hero subheads, post openings, social captions, email subject lines.

These are reference, not finished copy. Don't drop them into the live site without Strategy Claude shaping the surrounding paragraph.

1. "I shipped 10 apps in six months. I don't even know what a for-loop is."
2. "The bottleneck on software was never coding knowledge. It was knowing what to make."
3. "The priesthood is optional."
4. "Taste beats stack."
5. "Vibe coding is the lazy version of what's actually happening."
6. "The platform will tell you when to leave. Listen the first time."
7. "The .md files are the moat."
8. "Audit prompts beat audit checklists."
9. "Your taste is the input AI was missing."
10. "One project, finished, beats ten started."

See `docs/brand/anti-engineer-brief.md` for the worldview, persona modes (Heretic / Forager / Ghost Pirate), and voice rules these hooks operate inside.
