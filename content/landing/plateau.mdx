Discovery. A tool blows your mind. Two weeks of honeymoon. Then you start hitting walls, and you blame the tool. You drift away. A new tool comes out. Discovery again. Same loop.

I did this three times. Midjourney, ChatGPT, Base44. Each time I blamed the tool. Each time the real bottleneck was that nobody had ever shown me what the tool could actually do.

The only reason I got out was luck. A mentor entered my orbit. Most people don't get that person. They just quietly fizzle out.

This product is the person I wish I'd had three months ago.
